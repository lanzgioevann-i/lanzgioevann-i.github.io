Enrapture

With a heroic story and an adventure themed game, children who love to play games will enjoy this game. As you play this game, you familiarize yourself with numbers and mathematical equations, and without realizing it, you enhance your mathematical ability and you learn how to solve these mathematical equations repetitively. The good part about this is, this game challenges the player to solve the equation within 10 seconds and remember the answer as they dodge and avoid the obstacles. Correct numbers doesn’t spawn much often so it challenges the player to remember it for a long time. Answers to the equation varies with the difficulty selected by the player so even higher aged students that struggles and is having a hard time solving simple math equations mentally will be a perfect player in this game.
